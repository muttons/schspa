South Carolina Human Services Provider Association
Represents 56 Provider Agencies who support approximately 32,000 people with Intellectual and related disabilities with over 9,000 employees utilizing a self-reported $440,000,000.

Our Generous Sponsors

What we do

  • Support
  • Provide
  • Create

Support

Support individuals in the least restrictive environment with a focus on community inclusion and participation.

Provide

Provide opportunities for individuals with intellectual disabilities to have the opportunity to work and obtain gainful employment in their communities.

Create

Create environments that support innovation by sharing and learning from each other and by reaching out beyond our local borders to learn about new and best practice models of service.
